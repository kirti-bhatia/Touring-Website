Question

Draw a velocity versus time graph of a stone thrown vertically upwards and then coming downwards after attaining the maximum height.

Medium
Open in App
Updated on : 2022-09-05
Solution
Verified by Toppr

As the stone goes its velocity goes on decreasing ( it is moving against the gravity) and at the highest point i.e., maximum height its velocity become zero. Here, we have taken -u because in the upward motion velocity of stone is in upward direction and in the downward motion, the velocity is in downward direction.
